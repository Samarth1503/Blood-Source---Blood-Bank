FRESH FROZEN PLASMA(FFP) STORAGE

Fresh Frozen Plasma ( plasma separated from a unit of blood within 6-8 hrs of donation and rapidly frozen) is stored in Bank at -40 degree C or colder .It has a shelf life of one year. If not stored at this temp the coagulation factors as Factor VIII and Factor V deteriorate and the amount is greatly reduced thereby defeating the purpose for which it is to be administered.


FFP needs to be thawed before transfusion. in the blood centre in a plasma thawing bath. at 30 degree C - 37degree C which takes about 30-45 minutes FFP is transported in a blood transport box in which the temperature is maintained between +2 degree C to 6 degree C. Once thawed FFP is infused within 30 minutes.
