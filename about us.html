About Us

Welcome to Blood Source - your trusted partner in blood donation and transfusion services. Since our establishment in 1940s, we have been committed to providing safe and reliable blood products to hospitals and medical facilities in our community.

At Blood Source, we believe that every blood donation can save lives, and we strive to make the donation process as easy and convenient as possible for our donors. We have donation centers located throughout the area, as well as mobile blood drives that travel to schools, businesses, and community events. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always available to answer any questions and ensure a comfortable donation

Our

Mission

Providing life-saving blood products to those in need :

At our blood bank, our mission is to provide life-saving blood products to those in need. We are committed to maintaining a safe and reliable supply of blood products, including red blood cells, plasma, and platelets, and to ensuring that these products are available to patients whenever and wherever they are needed. Through our dedicated staff and volunteers, we strive to make the donation process as easy and comfortable as possible, and to build strong relationships with our donors and community partners.

Safety: the cornerstone of our practice

We take the safety of our blood supply seriously, and we adhere to the highest standards of quality and regulatory compliance. Our state-of-the-art laboratories and advanced testing techniques ensure that every unit of blood we collect is free of infectious diseases and meets the necessary specifications for transfusion.

We are committed to maintaining the highest safety standards at all times, and we welcome any questions or concerns you may have about our safety measures.

Advancing the science of transfusion medicine

As a leading blood bank organization, our mission is to advance the science of transfusion medicine through innovative research and development. We are committed to staying at the forefront of new discoveries and emerging technologies, and to sharing our knowledge and expertise with the wider healthcare community. By investing in the latest technology and equipment, and by collaborating with leading researchers and medical professionals, we aim to improve the safety, efficacy, and availability of blood products for patients around the world.
