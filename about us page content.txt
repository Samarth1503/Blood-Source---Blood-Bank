About Us page
Welcome to Blood Source - your trusted partner in blood donation and transfusion services. Since our establishment in 1999, we have been committed to providing safe and reliable blood products to hospitals and medical facilities in our community.

At Blood Source, we believe that every blood donation can save lives, and we strive to make the donation process as easy and convenient as possible for our donors. We have donation centers located throughout the area, as well as mobile blood drives that travel to schools, businesses, and community events. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always available to answer any questions and ensure a comfortable donation experience.

We take the safety of our blood supply seriously, and we adhere to the highest standards of quality and regulatory compliance. Our state-of-the-art laboratories and advanced testing techniques ensure that every unit of blood we collect is free of infectious diseases and meets the necessary specifications for transfusion.

Our mission is to save lives by providing a reliable and accessible blood supply, and we couldn't do it without the support of our generous donors and dedicated staff. We are proud to be a part of the local community and to serve the healthcare needs of our neighbors.

Thank you for considering Blood Source for your blood donation or transfusion needs. We look forward to serving you and helping to save lives together.



Advancing the science of transfusion medicine:
As a leading blood bank organization, our mission is to advance the science of transfusion medicine through innovative research and development. We are committed to staying at the forefront of new discoveries and emerging technologies, and to sharing our knowledge and expertise with the wider healthcare community. By investing in the latest technology and equipment, and by collaborating with leading researchers and medical professionals, we aim to improve the safety, efficacy, and availability of blood products for patients around the world.
