Our History
    • 1950s-1960s
    • 1970s-1980s
    • 1990s-2000s
    • 2010s-2020s
    • Blood Source was founded in 1956 by a group of doctors and community leaders who recognized the need for a centralized blood bank in the region. In the early years, the organization operated out of a small facility with limited resources, but with the help of dedicated volunteers and donors, we were able to grow and expand our services.
    • As the demand for blood products continued to grow, Blood Source expanded its operations, opening additional blood collection centers throughout the region. During this time, we also began to invest in new technologies and equipment to ensure the safety and quality of the blood products we provide.
    • In 1990s we introduced computerised donor management system, which streamlined our operations and improved our ability to track donations and communicate with donors. We also expanded our outreach efforts, partnering with local hospitals and community organizations to raise awareness about the importance of blood donation.
    • In recent years, Blood Source has continued to adapt and innovate in response to the changing needs of our community. We have implemented new safety protocols to protect our donors and staff during the COVID-19 pandemic, and we have expanded our services to include specialized blood products for patients with unique needs. We remain committed to providing a reliable and sustainable supply of blood and blood products to those in need, and we are grateful for the continued support of our donors and partners.
