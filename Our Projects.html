Blood Source is committed to providing a steady supply of blood for hospitals and patients in need across India. To achieve this goal, we propose a new initiative called "Rakta Yatra," which involves organizing a series of blood drives in every major city and town in India.

The project will involve several key steps, including:
1.Identifying key cities and towns across India
2.Partnering with local organizations such as schools, colleges, and community groups to organize blood drives in each identified location.
3.Recruiting and training volunteers
4.raise awareness about the importance of blood donation and encourage participation in the blood drives.
